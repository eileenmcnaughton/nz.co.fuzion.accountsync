This allows you to filter and search a list of all contacts which are "linked" to an accounts contact.

If you know what you are doing you can also make updates to specific values using the "Actions->Update Account Contacts". For example if you need to re-map a contact to another accounts contact you could update the "Accounts Contact ID" and then trigger the "Contact Pull" or "Contact Push" job to update that contact from/to the accounts system - you would also need to change "Accounts needs update" to 1 for the changes to be made.
